Students Robotics Interest Club "Bionik" started its activity in 2001 at the Faculty of Electronics and Information Technology, Warsaw University of Technology.

We gather students who want to broaden their knowledge of mobile and manipulation robotics. We carry out both individual and team projects — from tournament robots (LineFollower, Sumo, MicroMouse) to autonomous systems and educational platforms.

We organize competitions, workshops, and participate in numerous events promoting science and new technologies.
